jsSnow 0.5

Hey, what's this? Looks like XSnow!


Yes, it is. I was looking for a port of my favorite toy, but didn't find any. So I took the graphics from Rick Jansens Xsnow 1.40 (http://www.euronet.nl/~rja/), went to the kitchen, got me (Nikolaus Klepp, dr.klepp@gmx.at) a mixer, ice ... and here it is: the first XSnow port to JavaScript!

Breaking news: after 17 years here's version 0.5 .. now with trees :)

Where to download?

  • here's is the whole package of this page. Please refer to the html sources of how to install. It works for Mozilla based browsers (Netscape ...), Konqueror, Opera and M$IE. Also take a look at the CHANGES.
